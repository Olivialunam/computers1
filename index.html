Succulents

And where they grow.

  • A few types of popular succulant plants include=
  • Aloe Vera which can help heal and replinish hair.
  • Peyote, a spinless cactus.
  • Echeveria, usually used as decor in homes.
  • Even golden Asperagus

The regions in which they grow.

  • Aloe Vera is Native to Africa but can grow other places.
  • Peyote is native to Mexico and southwestern Texas.
  • Echeveria is usually found as a house plant because they are easlily maintained.
  • Asparagus can be grown many places but needs to be planted in early spring.

What exactly classifies a plant as a succulent?

  • Succulents are usually thicker plants that retain water. This can help in arid climates.
  • The word "succulent" means juice or sap.
